The series depicts the story of a fateful encounter between Do Do-hee (Kim Yoo-jung), a haughty but organised heiress of a conglomerate, and Jeong Gu-won (Song Kang), a demon who is cold and carefree that temporarily loses his powers. The couple embark on a contract marriage, agreeing to help each.

However, Ah-ri mentions how these influencers buy "followers", which tricks laypersons into believing that they are celebrities.
